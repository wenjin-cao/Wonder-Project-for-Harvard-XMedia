ABOUT US

Havard XMedia

We are a team of researchers at Harvard and practitioners from around the world who believe we can promote meaningful conversations about educational, ethical, and aesthetic topics through cross media storytelling. Learn more about our work at xmedia.gse.harvard.edu.


The WONDER Project

We designed this website for students all over the world to explore what happens when a story is told in one form, for example a book, is then told in another way,--like when a book becomes a movie. Exploring stories that go from one form of media to another is called cross-media. We invite you to explore cross-media with us through the story Wonder on this interactive website.
